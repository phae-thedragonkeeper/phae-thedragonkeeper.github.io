ozymandias

(november 2025) ... draft for zine idea?

bastard son of the first revolution
when machines weaved fiber and tied our fates
with iron rails and blood
now reify the shifting sands of the past
as you weave false futures

you’ve commodified divine creation itself
human words and art that fill the soul
now mix with iron and blood and
regurgitated into our stomachs

Oh
how little you must think of your fellow man
for you to believe they can be replaced by a mechanical parrot
with a million stolen faces

Maybe you will succeed
in you incessant chasing of a vague future
Maybe you and I will be crushed by this wheel of progress

But when machines break and burn
when your empire crumbles and falls
I wonder …

Who will sing a lament for our demise?
